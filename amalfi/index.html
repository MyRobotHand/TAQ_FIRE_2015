Vistas are painted blue and azure, iridescent green, lemon-yellow. Faraglioni cliffs and cascading macchia hang above the emerald water. The aromas of citrus fruit, sea, vanilla, freshly-filled puff pastries. And a volcano that silently admires all these wonders. We are in the south of the Italian Peninsula. We are in Heaven.
